How to be an Avid Reader

  1. Retrain your mind to be a book lover
  2. Admit that you know close to nothing.
  3. You must cultivate a deep desire to know more.
  4. Discipline yourself.
  5. Visit your local library
  6. Set a goal or target, like "i will finish my organic chemistry text in 2 days time".
  7. Be Consistent i.e read everyday.
  8. Make friends with people who read and study educative books regularly.
  9. Join a book club.
  10. Share what you have learnt with others.
