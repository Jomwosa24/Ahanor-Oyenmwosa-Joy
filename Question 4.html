Charlize Theron

Charlize Theron is a beautiful South African American actress that has won an Oscar award and many others including the Screen Globe award.This talented actress was born in South Africa, she speaks Afrikaans and English. She is a mother to two beautiful black girls whom she adopted few years back.She has starred in action packed movies such as Fast and Furious, Mad Fury, The Old Guard which was a major hits in 2020 and lots more.She was appointed as an ambassador of peacee by the United Nations in 2010. She has appeared in magazines including the famous American Ms magazine in 2004.
Time named her as one of the 100 most influential people on Earth.
